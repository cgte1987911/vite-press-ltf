### 癞格宝小王子

1. put an end to ... 结束...
   - put an end to this 结束这一切
   - put an end to me 了结我吧
   - I put an end to his pain
   - put an end to it
2. We're here to... 我们是来...的
   - We're here to help
   - We're here to win
   - We're here to celebrate
   - We're here to discuss
   - We're here to talk
3. How do you mange it 你是怎么做到的
   - How do you escape
   - How do you survive
   - How do you know that
4. I'm planning to  我打算...
   - I'm planning to try
   - I'm planning to ski
   - I'm planning to persuade her
   - I'm planning to take her out
