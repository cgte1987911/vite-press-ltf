1. My quest will take me through high winds and over high seas

   我的旅程将带我穿越狂风和汹涌的海洋。

2. Well isn't this a rare treat, Master Joao.

   哎呀，这可真是难得的款待啊，若昂大师

3. Whenever you have a problem,  just go on over to the cafe.

   无论你什么时候遇到问题，就去那家咖啡馆吧

4. I think I'd be lucky to get just one point out of five if I were fighting you, Father.

   我想如果和您交手的话，能从五分里拿到一分我就算走运了

5. Joao, don't knock textbooks. The advice of others can prove to be invaluable.

   若昂，别小看课本。别人的建议有时可能会非常宝贵

6. It's a difficult task, and there may  be hardships along the way, but  it's urgent that you find it.

   这是一项艰巨的任务，途中可能会遇到困难，但你必须尽快找到它。

7. I'm leaving you in charge of his education.  

   我把他的教育托付给你了。

8. Don't go easy on him because he's my son. Forge him into a man, Rocco. 别因为他是我儿子就对他手下留情，把他锻造成一个男人，罗科。

9. Come along now, swabbie.

   快跟上，小水手。

10. the whole town is in an uproar.

    整个小镇都乱成一团了。

11. shiver me timbers!

    哎呀我的天哪！（典型的海盗用语，常用来表达惊讶或震惊）

12. To tell the innkeeper what time you need to check out, just say no when they ask you if you want to check out at 8:00 AM. 

    要告诉旅店老板你想几点退房，只需在他们问你是否要在早上八点退房时回答“不”就行了。

13. That's because I've know the Duke since he was kneehigh to a grasshopper!

    那是因为我从公爵还是个小不点儿的时候就认识他了！

14. And whether foul or fair cause, I'm all for sending the boy out to learn the ways of the world.

    不管理由是好是坏，我都赞成把这男孩送出去见识世面。

15. There's nothing better than a hardy stint at sea to toughen a lad up.

    没有什么比在海上艰苦一段时间更能让小伙子变得坚强了。

16. This is the aquamarine tiara your father gave to me. 

    这是你父亲送给我的海蓝宝石王冠。

17. Ahoy there, matey, will ye be shoving off? 

    嗨，伙计，你是要启航了吗？

18. Well, it ought to last us about a month. But resting on that fact won't get us any more!

    嗯，这东西大概能撑我们一个月。不过光靠这个事实可不会让我们多得到一点！

19. You may need to loosen your pursestrings and treat the crowd if you want to persuade anyone to join our crew.

    如果你想说服别人加入我们的船员队伍，可能得大方一点，请大家喝一轮。

20. We rounded up 29 men. 我们凑齐了29个人。

21. Five cards without busting? You just tripled your stakes!

    五张牌还没爆点？你可是一举把赌注翻了三倍啊！

22. Under this grey head of mine is a noggin full of useful knowledge. Let me tell you about the guild.

    在我这颗灰白的脑袋下面，装满了有用的知识。让我来告诉你关于公会的事吧

23. It seems Portugal is out to get Turkey.

    看起来葡萄牙是要对付土耳其。

24. It seems Spain is cracking down on pirates.

    看起来西班牙正在打击海盗。
